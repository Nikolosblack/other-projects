About SoftC

Professional software development company

Our software development facilities are located in London, UK. Our team consists of such professionals as software engineers, developers, and scientists with rich experience in various technical domains. All company members have bachelor's or master's degrees in the computer sciences and at least seven years of expertise in the software development industry. We successfully combine our team spirit, mutual support, creative thinking, and our dedication to delivering the most efficient custom software solutions. We are committed to providing our clients with exceptional services.

To deliver value and high performance to clients with software products and technology innovations.

Principles that Guide Us

Responsibility

SoftC Company's team is responsible for the highest quality of services, practicability and efficiency of the decisions made during the development process, and for the realization of those obligations.

Professionalism

We are committed to the professional performance of our obligations, based on a profound knowledge and understanding of the software development process, rich experience, and compliance with safety.

Efficiency
