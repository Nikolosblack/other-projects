    SEO Audit Content Marketing Website Optimization

    SEO Audit

    A technical SEO audit is a detailed analysis of the site in order to identify technical errors. Your site is analyzed for a variety of parameters that directly or indirectly affect the number of targeted visitors from organic search.

    As a result of the audit, you receive not just a list of shortcomings, but full-fledged terms of reference and recommendations.

    Content Marketing

    Content marketing starts with a custom strategy. It includes creating useful and effective content and its distribution. High-quality content is one of the key effective stages of website promotion.

    The introduction of a content strategy will improve behavioral factors (user actions) and the relevance of the site, which will contribute to an increase in search positions.

    Website Optimization

    The website optimization includes internal and external SEO strategies, and a wide range of services: development of the site structure, compilation of the semantic core, content promotion, link building, setting up redirects, etc.

    SEO optimization is not a one-time job. It's a permanent process. Your website needs to be tuned and monitored day after day to achieve the set goals.
